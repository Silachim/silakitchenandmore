Our Story

More than snacks — we make memories.

Sila Kitchen and More was born out of a deep love for community, celebration, and food that truly brings people together. We believe the best moments in life deserve the best bites.

From intimate family gatherings to large events and corporate functions, we craft every piece of small chops with intentionality, freshness, and passion. No pre-made stock — everything is prepared fresh for your specific date.

We are proudly homegrown, built on the principle that Good Food truly makes for a Good Life.
